Fettuccine Alfredo

This is a classic fettuccine alfredo that does not use any cream. Instead, we create a velvety, luxurious sauce by combining starchy pasta water, butter, and parmesan cheese to create a nice emulsion. The result is a flavorful cream sauce that goes great with fettuccine or any wide bodied pasta noodle.

Ingredients

  • 8oz dried fettuccine
  • 1/2 to 3/4 cup pasta water
  • 5-6 Tbsp unsalted, high-fat butter
  • 75g grated parmesan

Steps

  1. Cook pasta in heavily salted water according to package instructions.
  2. Once pasta is cooked, transfer from water into saute pan along with any pasta water that comes with it.
  3. Add in another 1/2 cup of pasta water and toss.
  4. Add 5Tbsp utter and swirl with pasta to emulsify. Add in grated parm and stir to combine.
  5. Adjust consistency of sauce with extra pasta water, butter, or parmesan as necessary. Plate and serve with additional grated parm and fresh cracked black pepper.
